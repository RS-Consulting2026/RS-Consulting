Case Studies

Projects & Outcomes

A selection of project examples illustrating the types of challenges we help clients navigate. All cases anonymised.

Developer · Flood Zone 3a
Residential Development — EA Objection Resolved
Challenge

A developer's planning application had been stalled for eight months with an outstanding Environment Agency objection. The original FRA had underestimated climate change allowances and the Sequential Test was insufficiently evidenced.

Outcome

Revised FRA and Sequential Test produced. EA objection withdrawn within six weeks. Planning permission granted shortly after.

Architect · Mixed Use
Town Centre Regeneration — Integrated Drainage Design
Challenge

An architectural practice needed flood risk and drainage input at RIBA Stage 2 for a complex town centre regeneration scheme. Previous advisors had provided input too late, requiring costly revisions to the scheme layout.

Outcome

Appointed at Stage 2. Site levels and drainage strategy designed into the scheme from the start. Planning submission approved without flood risk objections.

Local Authority · SFRA
Strategic FRA Update — Local Plan Preparation
Challenge

A local planning authority required an updated Level 1 and Level 2 SFRA to support their emerging Local Plan, incorporating new flood mapping, updated climate change allowances, and revised housing growth allocations.

Outcome

SFRA delivered on programme. Adopted as part of the Local Plan evidence base. Inspector confirmed the flood risk evidence was thorough and up to date.
